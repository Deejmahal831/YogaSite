Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of oses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alightment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced student, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement -- you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restoative experience is suitable for students of and level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate and injury.

Relax Anytime with Savasana

Prepare yourself for Savasana. Lie down on your yoga mat with your side with palms up. Close your eyes and breathe slowly but deeply. Sink into the mat and let your worries slip away. When you are ready, roll on your side and use your arms to push yourself to a sitting position with legs crossed. Place your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that you have in life. Namaste.
